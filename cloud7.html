Meet Cloud 7

Cloud 7 is an official Filipino P-pop (Philippine Pop) boy group formed by Sparkle GMA Artist Center. Consisting of seven talented members aged 13-19, they made their initial debut on August 20, 2023, and officially launched on May 23, 2025. Known for their energetic dance-pop performances and youthful spirit, Cloud 7 is recognized as the youngest P-pop group under Sparkle's roster.

Also known as: C7, CLOUD 7
