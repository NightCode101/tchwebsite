What is Cloud 7?

Cloud 7 is an official Filipino P-pop (Philippine Pop) boy group managed by Sparkle GMA Artist Center. The seven-member group, ranging in age from 13 to 19 years old, debuted on August 20, 2023, and made their official media launch on May 23, 2025. Known for their dance-pop music and youthful energy, Cloud 7 is recognized as the youngest P-pop group under the Sparkle management roster.

Cloud 7 (2024): Fian, Kairo, Lukas, Egypt, PJ, Migz, and Johann

The Cloud House OG is an exclusive space designed for dedicated Cloud 7 fans and collaborators. Here, you can stay updated on the latest news, connect with other fans, participate in discussions, and be part of something bigger than yourself.

Our Story

The Cloud House OG was created on June 10, 2024 and was approved by Sir Jay Lyson and Tita Sarah Angeles. We were the first group to be approved at that time. Our community includes dedicated fans, supporters, and even some parents of Cloud 7 members who are passionate about the group's success.

We welcome all who share a passion for Cloud 7 and are ready to contribute positively to our growing community.
